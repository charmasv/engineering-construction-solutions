Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can you start a project?

We can typically begin most projects within 1-2 weeks of contract signing, depending on project complexity and current workload.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency services for critical electrical and mechanical systems. Call our emergency line for immediate assistance.

What areas do you serve?

We serve clients throughout South Africa.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. All our engineers are certified and our work meets industry standards.

How do you handle project pricing?

We provide detailed, transparent quotes with no hidden costs. Pricing can be fixed-price or time-and-materials, depending on project requirements.

What is your warranty policy?

We offer comprehensive warranties on all our workmanship and materials. Warranty periods vary by service type and are detailed in our contracts.
